Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about how mobile emissions testing works? We've got answers.

Do you do registration stickers on the spot?
We are currently working with the State of Utah to obtain licensing to distribute physical registration stickers on-site. In the meantime, immediately after passing your mobile test, we provide simple, instant digital instructions to complete your registration online so your tags arrive directly in your mail.
Do I need to sit and wait with my car during the test?
Not at all! You do not need to be present or wait outside. Simply hand us the keys or let us know where they are, and you can stay comfortable inside your home or office. The inspection takes 15 minutes or less, and we'll bring your keys right back along with your printed report.
What happens if my vehicle fails the emissions inspection?
If your car fails, our technician will print the diagnostic failure report, explain the trouble codes in plain terms, and suggest common repair paths. To make it completely stress-free, we provide one **complimentary retest within 15 days** once your repairs are complete.
